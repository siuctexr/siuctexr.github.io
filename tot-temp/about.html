Our Mission

  • Protect and preserve the Trail of Tears in Illinois and serve as a voice for this historic resource
  • Promote awareness of the historic legacy associated with the Trail of Tears
  • Work with the National Trail of Tears Association, federal and state agencies to develop, certify, and interpret sites
  • Continue to research and gather information related to the Trail of Tears experience
  • Offer outreach and educational opportunities related to the Trail
